Launch Approval Checklist (Template)
====================================

Model & Data
------------
- [ ] Training data documented and reviewed <!-- Data quality control -->
- [ ] Data consent/usage compliant with policy <!-- Legal compliance -->
- [ ] Dataset version recorded <!-- Traceability -->
- [ ] Baseline metrics and evaluation report complete <!-- Evidence of performance -->

Fairness & Safety
-----------------
- [ ] Fairness audit complete (metrics + gaps) <!-- Bias check -->
- [ ] Known limitations documented <!-- Transparency -->
- [ ] Safety risks assessed and mitigations listed <!-- Risk management -->

Security & Privacy
------------------
- [ ] Threat model reviewed <!-- Security review -->
- [ ] Secrets and credentials checked <!-- Secret hygiene -->
- [ ] PII handling reviewed <!-- Privacy control -->

Operations
----------
- [ ] Monitoring plan defined <!-- Post-launch observability -->
- [ ] Rollback plan documented <!-- Recovery plan -->
- [ ] Incident response contacts listed <!-- Escalation path -->

Approvals
---------
- [ ] Product Owner <!-- Business sign-off -->
- [ ] Engineering Lead <!-- Technical sign-off -->
- [ ] Legal/Compliance (if applicable) <!-- Regulatory sign-off -->
- [ ] Security (if applicable) <!-- Security sign-off -->
